The Best Books For Youth According to Yuki Truong

My Top 3 books for Youth

1/The Last Get Off, The First Rerite

A collection of short stories about work place, where much annoying problems originate. A work-place handbook for young people who do not have much work experience.

2/The Courage To Be Disliked

A Japanese phenomenon that teaches us the simple yet profound lessons required to liberate our real selves and find lasting happiness.

3/The Way We're Working Isn't Working

The Way We're Working Isn't Working is one of those rare books with the power to profoundly transform the way we work and live.
